Dec 2014-

Pastor Ibrahim Owino ochieng

Rev. Ibrahim Owino Ochieng is currently the Deputy Senior Pastor at Citam Kisumu Church.
He is married to Eunice Chepkurui Owino who is a co-pastor, and they are blessed with 3 destiny daughters namely Debora Achieng, Abigail Atieno and Shekainah Akoth
Pastor Ibrahim is passionate about souls and he believes that somebody should not die without hearing about Christ .He joined Citam Kisumu in the year 2009 as a student Pastor and by the grace of God he is currently the deputy senior Pastor at Citam Kisumu Church and oversees the following ministries which are doing well, Missions and Outreach, Counselling, visitation, face and administration.
Rev Ibrahim is a graduate of Nyangori Pentecostal Bible College, Vision International University and East Africa school of Theology, whereby he earned both Diploma and Degrees in Bible and Theology respectively.

His Ministry Philosophy is from 2 Corinthians 5:17

“Therefore, If anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation, Old things have passed away, behold all things have become new”.
Before joining Citam Kisumu, Pastor Ibrahim served as senior pastor at Bomet Pentecostal church for 10 years. He really thanks God for His faithfulness this far. Shalom.

Rev Walter Seth Openda

Rev. Walter O. Openda

Pastor Walter joined CITAM pastoral ministry in January 2003 as an Intern Pastor and was posted to the then Kisumu Pentecostal Church. He later served as Youth Pastor and was eventually appointed Deputy Senior Pastor at CITAM Kisumu Church in September 2010.
Walter is a graduate of Bible and Theology from the Nairobi Pentecostal Bible College [NPBC] and PAC University. He met and married his dear wife Rosemary Wambui while studying at NPBC and got married on 14th December 2002. Walter and Rosemary are blessed with two children; Sharlene (11) and Jeremy (8).
In the year 2013, Pastor Walter and his wife Rosemary were appointed and commissioned to pioneer the first CITAM Assembly in Europe located in Bucharest, Romania. Under his leadership, CITAM is now fully registered in Romania. He is currently developing a leadership team that will be instrumental in planting a solid ministry in Romania and beyond.
Walter is passionate about preaching and teaching as well as discipleship and leadership development. Planting CITAM Romania is the biggest project he has undertaken in his life and considers it both a blessing and challenging experience.
