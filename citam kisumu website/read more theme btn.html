Theme of the year

CITAM KISUMU

THEME: BEHOLD AN OPEN DOOR

Revelation 3:8
I know your deeds. See, I have placed before you an open door that no one can shut.

SUB-THEME

1. EXPLORE
2. OVERCOME OBSTACLES
3. TIME TO POSSESS

VISION

Committed believers possessing new grounds through obedience of God’s word.

MISSION

Committed to excellence

WORD FROM THE SENIOR PASTOR

The unveiled theme for Christ is the Answer Ministry for the year 2016 is “Behold an open door”, established from the foundational scripture of Revelation 3:8. As an assembly, our core objective is to build up our members in diverse areas of focus, which are instituted in our corporate theme, with implied focus on three principal areas; exploring the door, overcoming the presented obstacles, and lastly recognizing that this is the time set apart for us to possess.
To this end we want to express our sincere gratitude to our presiding Bishop, deputy Bishop, deacon and panel of elders, the entire CITAM Kisumu Pastoral team, members who contributed towards the Bible study book, compilation process as well as editing.
As Kisumu assembly, in humility and confidence we are moved by the strong belief that the compiled work is inspired by the Holy Spirit and will move all our members to further fellowship and an in-depth spiritual growth for effective service both within and outside our Church walls. Purchase you copy today.
Rev. Anataka Mugenyi
Senior Pastor - CITAM Kisumu
