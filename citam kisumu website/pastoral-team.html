Pastor Ruth Otieno CED

In this year 2016, the year of more than enough, let us position ourselves to partake and enjoy the blessings God has instore for us. This is the year of great expectations in our families, church, county and country atlarge. May Rev. 3:8 be a reality in our lives in Jesus Christ's name! On behalf of myself and my family we wish you a blessed year ahead. Amen.
Pastor Ruth Otieno is a born again Christian, joined Citam Kisumu Church on the 15th Nov. 2007. She is married to one Pastor Alfred Nyamiwa, who is currently pursuing his MDiv. Studies in theology at Emmanuel Christian Seminary in the USA. They are blessed with 3 children, 2 girls and a boy- namely: Neema , Naomi Shalom and Eliab Shammah months.She is in charge of the Christian Education Department and also oversees the women ministries and the welcome and hospitality ministry. Her Philosophy of ministry is seeing Christians being the instruments of spreading the fragrance of the knowledge of Christ throughout the world. - 2Cor. 2:14-15.

Missions/Outreach - Ibrahim O. Ochieng'

Pastor Ibrahim Owino ochieng

Rev. Ibrahim Owino Ochieng is currently the Deputy Senior Pastor at Citam Kisumu Church.He is married to Eunice Chepkurui Owino who is a co-pastor, and they are blessed with 3 destiny daughters namely Debora Achieng, Abigail Atieno and Shekainah Akoth
Pastor Ibrahim is passionate about souls and he believes that somebody should not die without hearing about Christ .He joined Citam Kisumu in the year 2009 as a student Pastor and by the grace of God he is currently the deputy senior Pastor at Citam Kisumu Church and oversees the following ministries which are doing well, Missions and Outreach, Counselling, visitation, face and administration.
Rev Ibrahim is a graduate of Nyangori Pentecostal Bible College, Vision International University and East Africa school of Theology, whereby he earned both Diploma and Degrees in Bible and Theology respectively.
His Ministry Philosophy is from 2 Corinthians 5:17
“Therefore, If anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation, Old things have passed away, behold all things have become new”.
Before joining Citam Kisumu, Pastor Ibrahim served as senior pastor at Bomet Pentecostal church for 10 years. He really thanks God for His faithfulness this far. Shalom.

Pastor Collins Muema Incharge of Youth Ministry

is the last born in a family of nine (4 boys and 5 girls). He gave his life to Christ at a teen’s camp in April 1996. While still a teenager he would be given opportunities to lead prayers in church service back in Makueni. He preached his first sermon in a youth service after graduating from class 8. Moreover, while in High School the Lord began to open more opportunities for him to minister to his fellow youth and students. However, his dream career was to be a medical doctor. He joined CITAM Ngong after its inception 2003 and continued to attend the youth services. In the years 2005 and 2006 he joined the youth leadership in the Ex-cans ministry. At the same time he also ministered in the music ministry as a choir member

In January 2006, he joined the Book Of Hope Kenya where he was trained on school evangelism. At the same month they began to reach out to the youth in the schools and colleges within Kajiado County with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. The Lord began to open opportunities for him to minister to the youth in high schools, colleges and universitities. It is at this time that his passion for Full time Pastoral ministry calling was brought to clarity through prayer and wise counsel given to him by pastors in CITAM Ngong, his mother (who is also a Pastor with Africa Inland Church) and other mentors.

Later in September (2006) Pastor Collins got an opportunity to pursue a Bachelors Degree in Bible and Theology at the Pan Africa Christian University (PACU) for 4 years. While at PACU he continued to serve and assist in the Youth Ministry at CITAM Ngong. At the same time he was involved in the PACU missions and outreach team – this helped expose him to opportunities both within and without the country (mostly in Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda). In his fourth year at PACU, he assumed the position of the University Mission Chairman until his graduation in August 2010. At this time he was able to get the missions’ team and other students to Bunia – Congo (DRC) for a 10 days missions outreach.

After his graduation, he continued to serve at the CITAM Ngong youth ministry as well as the CED department until 2011 March. Thereafter, the Lord opened an opportunity for him to serve for 22 months with the Deliverance Church Lang’ata as a Youth and Children’s Pastor. He later returned back to CITAM in January 2013 and is now serving at CITAM Kisumu as the Youth Pastor.

Pastor Collins met with the love of his life (Wandia Muciiri) in 2011 while serving at the Deliverance Church Lang’ata where she was involved in the Sunday school and hospitality ministries. They started dating in April 2012 and later got married on April 6th 2013.
