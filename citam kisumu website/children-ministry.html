Children Ministry

CITAM KISUMU

MISSION

The Mission of the Children’s Ministry is as that of Christ Is the Answer Ministry i.e. ‘To know God and to make Him known’ by equipping the children to be transformed into Christ’s image.

VISION

Transformed children; transforming society.

MOTTO

Teaching for transformation and growth.

CORE VALUES

CITAM children ministry core values include: Ministry, Leadership, Community, Family, Integrity, Accountability, Excellence, and Sanctity of life and Partnership.

OBJECTIVES

General Objective

To assist children learn and understand the word of God and at the same time build a strong children’s ministry structure of CITAM.

Specific Objectives

To teach and disciple the children on biblical principles and truths. To help the children understand and appreciate how to make these biblical truths and principles applicable in their everyday life. (Proverbs 22:6) Train a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not turn from it. To recruit, train and equip Sunday school teachers to be fellow workers with God to effectively teach and disciple the children To ensure a working partnership between parents and teachers through parents/ teachers fellowship (PTF). Provide skills and opportunities for children to reach out.

PURPOSE

The Children’s Ministry exists to supplement and complement the family in the effort to lay a spiritual foundation that in God’s timing will lead a child into a personal relationship with God through Jesus Christ, growth and involvement in service.

Children’s Ministry is the foundation of our church and nation. We are delighted to share with you about this department to partner with us as parents and guardians as we shape this current generation with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
