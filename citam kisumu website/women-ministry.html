CITAM KISUMU

VISION:

TO PROVIDE FELLOWSHIP AMONG WOMEN OF CITAM WHO WILL HAVE A BOND OF ONENESS WITH OTHERS AND BE EFFECTIVE IN MINISTRY

MISSION:

THROUGH INTENTIONAL DISCIPLESHIP, PRAYER, GROUP FELLOWSHIP, LOVE AND SERVICE FOR ONE ANOTHER, GOD WILL TRANSFORM US FROM ORDINARY TO EXTRA

ORDINARY WOMEN AFTER HIS OWN HEART.

PROFILE: CITAM Kisumu Women Ministry is a fellowship of ladies from ages 18 and above who are actively involved in various activities in church. The WM meets every 3rd Saturday of the month from 8.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. except in November when we have a CITAM joint meeting in Nairobi and December when we go on recess. The ladies are divided into ten groups named after mighty women of God in the bible namely: Truphena, Abigael, Dorcas, Priscillah, Loyce, Salome, Lydia, Eunice, Abigael and Phoebe. Besides, there is Tabitha Group that ministers to the Single Mums and Widows. The Women Ministry complements and supplements the specific ministry of the church and supports the church in prayer. We also support the Youth and Children Ministry by holding annual events such as the Youth Empowerment Day. We also encourage women in the recognition and development and use of their spiritual gifts for the glory and edification of the body of Christ.
