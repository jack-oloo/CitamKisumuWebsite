Youth Ministry

CITAM KISUMU

YOUTH MINISTRY PROFILE

Vision

To build up the youth through personal knowledge of God and discipleship in applied Christian faith and holy character for maximum impact to the world.

Our Mission: Why we exist?

a. To lead the youth into a definite experience of Salvation.
b. To disciple the youth towards Christian maturity.
c. To interpret the ethical implications of the Gospel of Jesus Christ with the aim of raising wholesome Christians.
d. To equip the youth with the Word of God and prepare them for world evangelism.
e. e) To provide opportunities to the youth for the development of interpersonal skills in Christian context.
f. To develop the youth into responsible church membership and patriotic citizenship.

Evangelism

Goal(s): To teach our youths on outreach; To fulfill the Great Commission in line with our church Vision; Model in Word and deed the Good News of Jesus Christ to all persons through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Discipleship

Goal(s): Daily growth of the youth in relationship with Christ through Bible Study and prayer to enhance Christian maturity.

Fellowship

Goal(s): Establishing a network of relationships for accountability; Foster unity in the Body of Christ – we are better together (Heb.10:25) We have 4 main youth fellowships: Teens, Ex-cans, Crossroads’ & Young Professionals/ Pacesetters.

Worship

Goal(s): Regular expression of oneself in awe of God; Reverence and adoration to God, for who He is and what He has done; enhance worship in truth and spirit We achieve the above through Youth and Teens Services every Sunday.

Service/Ministry

Goal(s): For the youths to discover their unique gifts, skills and abilities and nurturing them to glorify God. We have the various church service ministries in which we encourage our youths to participate, e.g., Praise & Worship, drama, dance, hospitality, missions, e.t.c.

A. A. Fellowship Ministries:

Goal(s): Establishing a network of relationships for accountability; Foster unity in the Body of Christ – we are better together (Heb.10:25)

1. 1. Men of Integrity (MOFI)

MOFI is an acronym of Men Of Integrity. MOFI is a body under the umbrella of Kisumu Pentecostal church youth ministry. It was formed on the 1st of October 2011 with the main aim of instilling moral principles among its members.It was also aimed at providing an avenue for young men to freely interact and discuss issues affecting them.
MOFI was officially launched at a mbuzi choma bush on 16th of October 2011 by the church leadership led by Senior Pastor Elias Mwaura at Membo's Millimani residence.

VISION

To develop young men to God's standard of moral and ethical behaviors. Romans 13:1-17; Ephesians 5:1-2.

MISSION

To empower young men to be morally and spiritually upright towards Christ's likeness. Ephesians 2:5.

OBJECTIVES

1. To provide an understanding of integrity to members.
2. To create a conducive atmosphere for members to bond.
3. To establish a mentoring program for members.
4. To sensitive members on their human and constitutional rights.
5. To create awareness on HIV and AIDS among the youth.
6. To instill a sense of responsibility among the young men.

MEMBERSHIP

Membership is free and open to all male youth from the ages of 18 to 35 years, and those within the same age brackets at heart.

MEETINGS

MOFI meetings are held every first Friday of the month followed by the hot seat every third Sunday of the month.

1. 2. Ladies of Virtue (LOV)

This is an umbrella fellowship for all young ladies between the ages 13-35 years. It is a mentorship platform that offers transitional counsel from teenage to adulthood. It is led by Sis. Margaret Juma.

1. 3. Pacesetters

This is a fellowship of youth and single people in the ages 25-35 years old. They meet every second Saturday of the month. They also plan and attend conferences, retreats. Mostly, this fellowship attracts those that want to settle in life. Currently, led by Everastus Obura.

1. 4. Ex-cans

This is group of form four graduants who meet every Tuesday and Thursday from 2-5pm at the church compound. On Tuesday they touch base, play games and spend quality time (1 hour) in Bible Study. On Thursday, they have topical discussions on issues that affect them. The class of 2013 is led by Dorcas Kimutai.

1. 5. Cross-roaders’

This fellowship attracts mostly college students who meet every Sunday from 11am-1pm after the youth service. Their program entails a moment of worship and prayer, devotion/Bible study and then quality time for discussion as they address on issues affecting them in the 21st century. Currently, it is led by Beryl Otieno. B. Service Ministries:

Goal(s): For the youths to discover their unique gifts, skills and abilities and nurturing them to glorify God.

1. 1. Drama

This is very creative and art ministry within the church. Membership is on spiritual gifts of poetry, art and singing. They meet thrice in a week for practice. Currently, it is led by Harncel Oyango who is the outgoing chairman.

1. 2. Dance

This is a very essential ministry by the youth. It is based on gifting and talents. They minister through songs and dances in most if not all the youth functions and services. They meet thrice a week for practice. Currently it is led by Pauline Muthoni.

1. 3. Leadership

In the CITAM Youth Ministry, we have several categories of youth leaders: Youth Pastor, Youth Patron, Youth Executive, Youth committees, and Youth workers. All these compose our leadership structures. The Youth Pastor provides the overall leadership and direction to the youth ministry. The Youth workers serve on voluntary basis and help in facilitating the youth ministries mostly as they offer mentorship to the youth. They are led by the Youth Patron (currently Dr. Jackson Muhirwe). Moreover, we have youth committees composed of young people in charge of different youth departments, fellowships, services in line with their spiritual gifts and talents. The committees are headed by Coordinators who compose the 9 Youth Executive leaders of the youth ministry and chaired by the Youth Pastor.

1. 4. Praise & Team/choir

This is a very crucial team in our ministry. They spend time together twice in the week to pray, study the word together and practice on songs laid to their hearts by the Spirit of God to ensure that our worship to God is in truth and spirit. Currently, we have the Teens worship team of 7 worshippers led by Dorcas Kimutai while in the youth service the team is combined with the family service team to make a total of 40 worshippers led by Dr. Oyaro.

1. 5. Ushering

This is a team of youth who take care of people’s needs during and after the services. They ensure that those who attend our services are comfortable with good places to sit and control movements during the services. They also spend time with the first time visitors to welcome them and serve them with soft drinks and snacks as they acquaint the visitors on more information about CITAM Kisumu Youth ministry. In the youth service the team is led by Fred Olware while in the Teens service we have Barnett (an Ex-can).

1. 6. Sound

To create a conducive environment of worship for the youth a quality public address system and musical instruments are unavoidable. Therefore, this has become a key ministry in the teens and youth services every Sunday and so we have young people trained to monitor the music systems and ensure quality sound equalization. Currently, we have a crew of youth: Harncell, Pauline, Shadrack, Philemon, Bens, Nelson who work closely with Brian (Sound Technician).

1. 7. Outreach & Missions

Coordinator: Mike Messo

Goal(s): To teach our youths on outreach; To fulfill the Great Commission in line with our church (CITAM) Vision; Model in Word and deed the Good News of Jesus Christ to all persons through the power of the Holy Spirit.
Scope: Our target group is the youth is school and at the market place between the ages 13 – 35 years. The schools include: primary, high school, college/university and young professionals.
Mode: To reach the youth through media, open air, concerts, weekend challenges.

C. Nurture Ministries:

Goal(s): Regular expression of oneself in awe of God; Reverence and adoration to God, for who He is and what He has done; enhance worship in truth and spirit.

1. 1. Teens Service

Target group: The service is composed of teenage youth of ages 13-19 years.
Scope: It is a two-hour fully-fledged service. We celebrate the Lord’s Table (Holy Communion) every first Sunday of the month. We begin with an intercessory hour from 10.30-11.00, then spend quality time in praise and worship, offertory, talent ministries, exhortation and benediction. We have services every Sunday from January – December 31stunless we have special combined services with the youth and family services. Mainly the service begins at 11.00am – 1.00pm.

1. 2. Youth Service

Target Group: This service comprises of youth in the ages between 20-35 years.
Scope: This is a vibrant youthful expression service that runs every Sunday for one and half hours from 8am-930am promptly. It calls for maximum youth participation. This service entails the following program: Praise & Worship, intercessory prayer, offertory, and inspired preaching. During the service we also encourage youth to minister through their diverse talents and gifts (e.g., drama, dance); this is to ensure that these gifts are exploited appropriately and in the right place – the body of Christ for edification. We also celebrate the Lord’s Table (Holy Communion) every first Sunday of the month.
Youth Ministry is deﬁned as the response of the Church to the needs of young people, fostering their faith in Jesus Christ and their communion with the Blessed Trinity, and drawing them into responsible participation in the life, mission and ministry of the Church.
Youth Ministry is a series of programs and activities to give young people a chance to participate in Church life, develop their own faith and spirituality, and discover new friendships.
We Invite all between the ages of 13 years-35 years to get involved in the youth programs by participating in the following fellowships by age-group.
1. Ages 13 – 18: Teens Ministry
2. Ages 19 – 24: Cross Roads Fellowship
3. Ages 25 – 35: Young Professionals Fellowship
